About Us

Termin helps you organize your meetings. Improve the hour of meeting when exist guests around the world and it's difficult to set a good hour. See how you can create meetings in a simple way and share the meeting with your guests.

Why we are best
for you

We help you organize your meetings, proposing you a good hour when have conflicts with time zones of your guests, and so make possible the meeting.

Wherever, Anytime

Wherever and anytime you want, you can begin to create a new meeting, regardless you are connected, the application saves the information locally and when it's connected, upgrade the information of meeting.

Good hour for your meeting

Always it's difficult to select a good hour in meetings with guests around the world. We propose you a solution in the hour of your meeting, agree with the time zones.

Share the meeting

You can share the meeting before you confirm the new meeting, or send invitations of the meeting to whoever you want.
